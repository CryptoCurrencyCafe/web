Title: Using Materials
Date: 2015-01-10
Author: David Evans

I am delighted when people want to use or adapt the course materials
we have developed, and all materials we developed for this course
as available under a [Creative Commons Attribution-Noncommercial-Share
Alike 3.0 United States 
License](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/3.0/us/).  (Some
materials may come from other courses, and will be licensed differently,
as noted.)

I hope you will be able to take advantage of this material in your own
courses, and would be happy to help with this.

If you do use these materials, please:

- Send email to <a href="mailto:evans@cs.virginia.edu"><em>evans@cs.virginia.edu</em></a>
explaining how you are using the materials (include a URL if
applicable).

- Include a suitable credit.  Some materials include Credits at the
bottom, please include and adapt them as 
necessary if you use this material.  Most other materials were developed
by David Evans and should be credited with something like,
&#8220;Adapted from materials developed by David Evans&#8221;.

If you would like to use these materials for [*commercial purposes*](http://mirrors.creativecommons.org/defining-noncommercial/Defining_Noncommercial_fullreport.pdf),
permission is required.  To request permission, send email to [_evans@cs.virginia.edu_](mailto:evans@cs.virginia.edu).
