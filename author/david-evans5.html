Class 1: Why are these buildings burning down?

Posted: Mon 12 January 2015

PDF version for printing

Schedule

Before 11:59pm tomorrow (Tuesday, 13 January):

  1. Read: Course Syllabus

  2. Read: The Magic of Mining, The Economist, 10 January 2015.

  3. Read: Chapter 1: Introduction and Chapter 2: How Bitcoin Works from Andreas M. Antonopoulos, Mastering Bitcoin: Unlocking Digital Cryptocurrencies book (also available in print).

  4. Setup a bitcoin wallet (if you don't already have one, MultiBit is a good choice for this). After creating a wallet, you should find the Recieving Address, to use in PS0. Note that it is not necessary now, or at any time during this class, for you to actually buy bitcoins with your own money. If you are inclined to do this, be careful, and don't use any funds you cannot afford to lose!

  5. Submit Problem Set 0 before 11:59pm tomorrow (Tuesday, 13 January).
