Using Materials

I am delighted when people want to use or adapt the course materials we have developed, and all materials we developed for this course as available under a Creative Commons Attribution-Noncommercial-Share Alike 3.0 United States License. (Some materials may come from other courses, and will be licensed differently, as noted.)

I hope you will be able to take advantage of this material in your own courses, and would be happy to help with this.

If you do use these materials, please:

  • Send email to evans@cs.virginia.edu explaining how you are using the materials (include a URL if applicable).

  • Include a suitable credit. Some materials include Credits at the bottom, please include and adapt them as necessary if you use this material. Most other materials were developed by David Evans and should be credited with something like, “Adapted from materials developed by David Evans”.

If you would like to use these materials for commercial purposes, permission is required. To request permission, send email to evans@cs.virginia.edu.
